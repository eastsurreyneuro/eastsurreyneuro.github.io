About Me

Hi, I'm Lisa.

I'm a specialist neuro physiotherapist working across East Surrey. I have built up extensive experience in acute, inpatient, neuro-rehab, and community settings over the past 11 years, throughout Surrey and London.

I am currently working in an NHS outpatient setting at the internationally renowned National Hospital for Neurology and Neurosurgery, Queen Square.

I have a wealth of knowledge, skills, and experience in treating a variety of neurological conditions including multiple sclerosis, spinal cord injury, stroke, brain injury, Guillain-Barré syndrome, motor neurone disease, Parkinson’s disease, neuromuscular conditions, and other progressive neurological conditions.

I am HCPC registered, a member of the Chartered Society of Physiotherapy (CSP), and the Association of Chartered Physiotherapists Interested in Neurology (ACPIN).

Services

I offer specialist neuro physiotherapy assessments and treatments for anyone with a neurological condition in the East Surrey area. Assessment and treatments are carried out within your own home.

Each session is tailored to your specific needs and begins with an in-depth assessment. This includes understanding your neurological diagnosis, past medical history, and what you hope to achieve from the physiotherapy sessions. Based on this, we will compile a tailored programme specifically designed to meet your needs.

This may include any of the following:

  • Advice and education
  • Strengthening and stretching programs
  • Hands-on treatment
  • Balance re-training and education
  • Spasticity management
  • Positioning and/or seating advice

Frequently Asked Questions

Where will the physiotherapy session take place?

I will visit you at your home.

Do I need a referral from my GP?

No, you do not need a referral from a GP or consultant. I offer a self referral service. Please email me with a short message, I will then ring you back to organise a home visit.

How much will it cost?

Initial assessment (90 mins) - £110.
Each follow up session (60 mins) - £90.

What forms of payment do you accept?

Fees are paid at the end of each session, via card.

Do you work with medical insurance companies?

No, I am unable to offer this service at this time.
